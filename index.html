    How to use the Todo App

    Step1: Enter the task in text area.
    Step2: Click on Add button. Your list task will add in the listed below.
    Step3: Make your List of daily tasks here.
    Step4: Mark the task completed by clicking on done icon,
    Step5: If you want to edit some task, you can edit it by clicking on edit/pencil icon. Prompt will appear on your screen. Enter the task into an input field and then either you can hit enter or clicking on ok button. Your text will be updated.
    Step6: If you want to delete, click on delete icon to delete the task.
